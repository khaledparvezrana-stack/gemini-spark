03 — How It Works

From Assistant → Active Partner

Sundar Pichai calls Spark "the next evolution of smart digital assistants." Here's the architecture.

The Antigravity Engine

Spark is built from Gemini base models plus an agentic harness from Google Antigravity. This orchestration layer enables long-horizon task planning with minimal human oversight — a fundamental shift from prompt-response to goal-oriented execution.

Step 1

You set a goal

"Send my boss a status update with data from all my project docs."

Step 2

Spark gathers context

Pulls facts from Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides autonomously.

Step 3

Drafts & executes

Writes the email draft, presents it for your review, then sends.

Step 4

Keeps working

Continues monitoring your inbox and tasks — even when your laptop is closed.
